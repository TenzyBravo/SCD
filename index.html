Who We Are

Our Purpose

Guided by integrity, compassion, and evidence — we design programs that put communities first.

🎯

Mission

To empower youth through skills, education, and health awareness, while uplifting vulnerable children with comprehensive support, protection, and opportunities to thrive.

🌍

Vision

A future where every Zambian youth is empowered to drive sustainable development and every vulnerable child feels safe, valued, and empowered to reach their full potential.

💚

Core Principle

Non-profit, non-political, and non-discriminatory — improving the lives of vulnerable children, empowering youth, and fostering sustainable community development.

Flagship Programmes
Our Programmes

Ten flagship programmes. Real results.

Community-led, evidence-based interventions transforming the lives of children, adolescents, young people, women, parents, and communities across Zambia.

Menstrual Health

My Fabulous Natural Abilities

1,000+ adolescent girls & young women reached with menstrual health, hygiene, and self-confidence education. 84 girls received reusable sanitary kits to protect dignity and school attendance.

Mental Health

The Serene Mind

5 mental health programmes implemented across 5 communities, reaching 1,000+ adolescents and young people with awareness, psychosocial support, and stigma reduction.

Education

Education Support

850 learners supported with essential educational materials — reducing barriers, improving attendance, and boosting academic participation among vulnerable children.

Youth

Youth Leadership & Empowerment

50+ youth dialogue and capacity-building sessions equipping hundreds of young people with leadership, life skills, entrepreneurship, and civic engagement.

Climate

Climate Change Education

5 schools · 350 children reached with climate adaptation, tree planting, waste management, and sustainable environmental practices.

Parenting

My Child, My Mirror

120 parents and caregivers mentored on positive parenting, child protection, safeguarding, and creating safe, nurturing home environments.

SRHR

I Am Me

13 communities reached with Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights education — promoting informed decision-making, gender equality, and prevention of teenage pregnancies.

Health

Your Health, Your Passport to the Future

34 communities sensitised on HIV prevention & testing, STIs, cervical cancer awareness, and healthy lifestyle practices.

WASH

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

8 communities · 30 schools sensitised on safe hygiene practices, disease prevention, and improved sanitation — with hygiene products for participating schools.

Enrolment

Back-to-School Campaigns

10 Back-to-School Campaigns mobilising communities and supporting vulnerable children to enrol and stay in school.
